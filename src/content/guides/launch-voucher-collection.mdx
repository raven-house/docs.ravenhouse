# Launch a Voucher Collection

Launch your USDC voucher collection on **Raven House**, a privacy-first platform on the **Aztec Layer 2 blockchain** (live on testnet). Using zero-knowledge proofs, Raven House enables private minting and claiming of vouchers, keeping wallet addresses and voucher details confidential.

This guide walks you through launching a voucher collection for airdrops, events, or rewards, from setup to funding.

## Step 1: Set Up Your Collection

Define your voucher collection’s identity, economics, and minting strategy on the Raven House platform.

### 1.1 Define Your Collection

Navigate to the **Launch Collection** page and select the **Voucher Collection** tab. Enter the core details.

**Example: Starlight Rewards Collection**

- **Collection Name**: Starlight Rewards
- **Voucher Amount**: 50 USDC per voucher (redeemable value)
- **Description**: “Starlight Rewards offers 2,000 USDC vouchers for exclusive event access and community rewards, redeemable privately on Aztec.”

![Define Collection](/img/docs/voucher-collection/voucher-collection-details.png)

> [!TIP]
>
> Use a clear, compelling description to highlight your voucher’s purpose and value.

### 1.2 Choose Voucher Background

Select or upload a background image for your vouchers to reflect your brand.

- **Option 1: Pre-Designed Templates**  
  Choose from Raven House’s professional voucher templates.  
  ![Voucher Templates](/img/docs/voucher-collection/voucher-background-templates.png)

- **Option 2: Custom Background**  
  Upload a PNG with:
  - **Recommended Dimensions**: 334 × 207 pixels
  - **Alternative**: Multiples (e.g., 668 × 414) with the same aspect ratio  
    ![Custom Background](/img/docs/voucher-collection/upload-custom-voucher-background.png)

### 1.3 Set Economics and Supply

Configure the financial and supply rules.

**Example Settings for Starlight Rewards**:

- **Mint Price**: 0 USDC (free for airdrops)
- **Max Supply**: 2,000 vouchers (fixed for exclusivity)
- **Mint Limit per Wallet**: 3 vouchers to ensure fair distribution

![Economics & Supply](/img/docs/voucher-collection/voucher-economics-form.png)

> [!TIP]
>
> Set a low or zero mint price for promotional vouchers to boost adoption.

### 1.4 Plan Minting Stages

Control who can mint vouchers and when.

#### Enable Public Minting

- **Toggle On**: Allows public and whitelisted minting stages.
- **Toggle Off**: Restricts to whitelisted addresses only.

![Public Minting Toggle](/img/docs/whitelist-mint/public-minting-toggle.png)

#### Add Whitelist Stages

Click **Add Whitelist Stage**:

- **Mint Price**: 0 USDC (free for community members)
- **Start Date & Time**: Sept 1, 2025, 10:00 AM UTC
- **Duration**: 2 days
- **Example**: Starlight Rewards whitelist for early supporters.

![Whitelist Stage](/img/docs/voucher-collection/configure-whitelist-stage.png)

#### Add Public Mint Stages

Click **Add Public Mint Stage**:

- **Mint Price**: 5 USDC (small fee for public)
- **Start Date & Time**: Sept 3, 2025, 10:00 AM UTC
- **Duration**: 5 days
- **Example**: Open minting for Starlight Rewards after whitelist phase.

![Public Mint Stage](/img/docs/voucher-collection/configure-public-mint-stage.png)

#### Minting Strategies

- **Early Access**: Whitelist (Sept 1–2), then public (Sept 3–7).
- **Free Airdrop**: Whitelist at 0 USDC, public at 5 USDC, running concurrently.
- **Sequential Phases**: Whitelist only (Days 1–2), then public only (Days 3–7).

![Mint Stages Overview](/img/docs/voucher-collection/mint-stages-overview.png)

> [!NOTE]
>
> After setup, manage whitelist addresses. See [Whitelist Management Guide](/guides/whitelist-management).

## Step 2: Deploy Collection

Deploy your voucher collection to the Aztec testnet, making it ready for minting and claiming.

1. **Review Settings**:
   - Confirm name, voucher amount (50 USDC), supply (2,000), and stages.
2. **Deploy**:
   - Click **Deploy Collection** on the **Launch Collection** page.
   - Review the confirmation pop-up, as deployment is irreversible.

![Deploy Collection](/img/docs/voucher-collection/launch-voucher-collection.png)

> [!TIP]
>
> Ensure your Azguard or Obsidion wallet is connected with testnet tokens.

---

## Step 3: Fund and Manage

Fund your collection to enable private voucher claims and manage it post-launch.

### 3.1 Fund Your Collection

Navigate to the funding page to deposit USDC for claims.

- **Total Funding Needed**: 100,000 USDC (50 USDC × 2,000 vouchers)
- **Funding Options**:
  - **Partial**: Fund 25% (25,000 USDC) initially, add more as needed.
  - **Full**: Fund 100% upfront for seamless claiming.
  - **Progressive**: Start with 10,000 USDC, increase based on minting activity.

![Fund Collection](/img/docs/voucher-collection/fund-voucher-collection.png)

> [!NOTE]
> Users can only claim vouchers while funds are available. Top up as needed.

### 3.2 Manage Post-Launch

- **Monitor Funds**: Track remaining funds and claiming activity.
- **Whitelist Management**: Add addresses and enable whitelist. See [Whitelist Management Guide](/guides/whitelist-management).
- **Engage Community**: Use Raven House’s Discord bot to notify users of voucher activity.

## Tips for Success

### Planning Your Launch

- **Time Zones**: Schedule mint stages for your audience’s time zones (e.g., UTC for global access).
- **Marketing**: Promote Starlight Rewards on Discord and X to drive engagement.
- **Buffer Time**: Allow 1–2 days for funding transaction confirmations.

### Voucher Design

- Use predesigned backgrounds created by Raven House team.
- Test templates with a small batch to ensure visual quality.

### Troubleshooting

- **Deployment Fails**: Verify wallet connection and testnet token balance.
- **Funding Issues**: Ensure sufficient USDC in your wallet.
- **Whitelist Problems**: Confirm whitelist is enabled and addresses are added.
- **Need Help?** Join our [Discord community](https://discord.gg/CR8nKg5gYB) or contact support via Raven House.
