# Fix Popup Blocker Issues

If you're getting an error like "Failed to open popup. It may have been blocked by the browser" when trying to mint NFTs, your browser is blocking wallet popups. Here's how to fix it:

## Quick Fix Links

**For Chromium-based browsers (Chrome, Brave, Edge), paste these URLs directly in your address bar:**

- **Brave**: `brave://settings/content/popups`
- **Chrome**: `chrome://settings/content/popups`  
- **Edge**: `edge://settings/content/popups`

Then add `https://app.obsidion.xyz/` to the "Allowed to send pop-ups and use redirects" section.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### ✅ Google Chrome

1. Open Chrome
2. Click the 3-dot menu (top-right corner) → **Settings**
3. In the left sidebar, click **Privacy and security**
4. Click **Site settings**
5. Scroll down and click **Pop-ups and redirects**
6. Choose either:
   - **Sites can send pop-ups and use redirects** (to allow all)
   - **OR** under "Customized behaviors", add `https://app.obsidion.xyz/` under **Allowed to send pop-ups and use redirects**

### ✅ Brave Browser

1. Open Brave
2. Click the 3-line menu (top-right) → **Settings**
3. Go to **Privacy and security** → **Site and Shields Settings**
4. Click **Pop-ups and redirects**
5. Toggle to **Allow** or add `https://app.obsidion.xyz/` to allowed sites

### ✅ Mozilla Firefox

1. Open Firefox
2. Click the 3-line menu (top-right) → **Settings**
3. Click **Privacy & Security** from the sidebar
4. Scroll to **Permissions** → look for **Block pop-up windows**
5. Either:
   - **Uncheck** "Block pop-up windows" to allow all
   - **OR** click **Exceptions...** and add `https://app.obsidion.xyz/`

### ✅ Microsoft Edge

1. Open Edge
2. Click the 3-dot menu (top-right) → **Settings**
3. Navigate to **Cookies and site permissions**
4. Scroll down and click **Pop-ups and redirects**
5. Set to **Allow** for all sites **OR** add `https://app.obsidion.xyz/` under **Allow** section

### ✅ Safari (macOS)

1. Open Safari
2. Go to top menu → **Safari** > **Settings** (or Preferences)
3. Go to the **Websites** tab
4. Select **Pop-up Windows** in the sidebar
5. For `app.ravenhouse.xyz`, choose **Allow** from the dropdown

## Still Having Issues?

If you're still experiencing problems after following these steps:

1. **Refresh the page** after changing settings
2. **Clear your browser cache** and try again
3. **Temporarily disable ad blockers** (they might interfere)
4. **Try a different browser** to isolate the issue
